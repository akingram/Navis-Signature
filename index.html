Our Story

For over eight years, the Navis Signature name has been synonymous with uncompromising quality and artisanal excellence in footwear craftsmanship.

Where Tradition Meets Innovation

Founded in 2018 by master craftsman Marvellous, our workshop began as an operation in the heart of Africa. Today, while we've grown in reputation and reach, we remain committed to the principles that defined us from the beginning.

Each pair of Navis Signature shoes represents over 10-12 hours of meticulous handwork, utilizing techniques passed down through generations while incorporating modern advancements in comfort and sustainability.

We source only the finest materials from ethical suppliers around the world, ensuring that every component meets our exacting standards for quality, durability, and aesthetic appeal.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Africans and individuals globally by creating sustainable job opportunities and platforms for growth. Through innovation, ethical manufacturing, and sustainable practices, we are committed to developing talent, empowering youth, and helping people discover and fulfill their God-given purpose while taking African-made leather footwear to the world.

Our Vision

To establish a world-class manufacturing environment dedicated to producing premium, comfortable, and durable leather footwear for adults, teenagers, and children. Our vision is to ensure that every household in Africa and ultimately across the globe proudly wears Navis footwear for all occasions. Over the next 10 years, Navis Signatures aims to grow from Accra to every continent, becoming a globally recognized symbol of African excellence, craftsmanship, and heritage in leather footwear.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and craftsmanship process.

How long does it take to create a pair of Navis Signature shoes?
Each pair of Navis Signature shoes requires between 10-12 hours to complete, depending on the complexity of the design and materials. Bespoke orders may take 2-4 weeks as they involve multiple fittings and custom creation.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship worldwide. All international orders are shipped via express courier. Delivery times vary by location but typically range from 2-5 business days after completion.
What makes Navis Signature products different from other luxury brands?
Our products are entirely handcrafted by master artisans using traditional techniques that have been refined over generations. We use only the finest materials, and each item undergoes rigorous quality control. Unlike many luxury brands, we maintain complete control over our production process from start to finish.
How should I care for my Navis Signature products?
We recommend using proper storage methods when not using your products. Clean them regularly with a soft brush or cloth, and use conditioners appropriate for the specific material. We offer a complimentary care guide with every purchase.
Do you offer a repair service?
Yes, we provide a comprehensive repair and refurbishment service for all Navis Signature products. This includes sole replacement, restitching, reconditioning, and more. We strive to extend the life of your investment pieces for many years to come.
