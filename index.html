Our Story

For over three years, the Navis Signature name has been synonymous with uncompromising quality and artisanal excellence in footwear craftsmanship.

Where Tradition Meets Innovation

Founded in 2023 by master craftsman Marvellous, our workshop began as an operation in the heart of africa. Today, while we've grown in reputation and reach, we remain committed to the principles that defined us from the beginning.

Each pair of Navis Signature shoes represents over 200 hours of meticulous handiwork, utilizing techniques passed down through generations while incorporating modern advancements in comfort and sustainability.

We source only the finest materials from ethical suppliers around the world, ensuring that every component meets our exacting standards for quality, durability, and aesthetic appeal.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create exceptional footwear that combines timeless artistry with modern comfort, preserving traditional craftsmanship while innovating for the future. We are committed to sustainable practices, ethical sourcing, and creating products that stand the test of time in both quality and style.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as the pinnacle of artisanal footwear, where each pair tells a story of heritage, passion, and unparalleled attention to detail. We envision a world where quality triumphs over quantity, and where consumers value the story behind the products they wear.

Our Craftsmanship Process

Every pair of Navis Signature shoes undergoes a meticulous creation process that blends time-honored techniques with innovative approaches.

01

Material Selection

We source only the finest leathers and materials from trusted suppliers around the world, ensuring exceptional quality and sustainability.

02

Pattern Cutting

Each design is carefully patterned by hand, paying meticulous attention to grain direction and leather characteristics.

03

Hand Stitching

Our artisans employ traditional stitching techniques that allow for flexibility and durability that machine stitching cannot replicate.

04

Lasting & Soling

The upper is carefully stretched over a last and attached to the sole using techniques that ensure perfect fit and comfort.

05

Finishing

Each shoe undergoes multiple stages of polishing and finishing to achieve the distinctive Navis Signature appearance.

06

Quality Assurance

Every pair is rigorously inspected to ensure it meets our exacting standards before being prepared for delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and craftsmanship process.

How long does it take to create a pair of Navis Signature shoes?
Each pair of Navis Signature shoes requires between 4-8 weeks to complete, depending on the complexity of the design and materials. Our bespoke service may take longer, typically 8-12 weeks, as it involves multiple fittings and custom last creation.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship worldwide. All international orders are shipped via express courier with full tracking and insurance. Delivery times vary by location but typically range from 2-5 business days after completion.
What makes Navis Signature shoes different from other luxury brands?
Our shoes are entirely handcrafted by master artisans using traditional techniques that have been refined over generations. We use only the finest materials, and each pair undergoes rigorous quality control. Unlike many luxury brands, we maintain complete control over our production process from start to finish.
How should I care for my Navis Signature shoes?
We recommend using high-quality shoe trees when not wearing your shoes. Clean them regularly with a soft brush or cloth, and use conditioners and polishes appropriate for the specific leather. Avoid wearing the same pair consecutive days to allow them to air properly. We offer a complimentary care guide with every purchase.
Do you offer a repair service?
Yes, we provide a comprehensive repair and refurbishment service for all Navis Signature shoes. This includes sole replacement, heel repair, reconditioning, and more. We strive to extend the life of your investment pieces for many years to come.
